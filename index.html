What is The Machine?

This story takes place in the futureistic version of the century. Evrybody relys on technology for everything that they do. It begins as their source of habit that they don't verbally communicate with other people. This includes:

Are We Facing This Problem Now?

With technology being improved everyday, we rely so much more on them for communication, navigation, transportation, enterainment and even asking the virtial assistance for our commands. Although these functions are in great use for us, it can also be the end of physical conatct and exercises from the real world.
